Greek Salad

Description

A fresh and colorful Greek salad with crisp veggies, creamy feta cheese, and a light olive oil dressing. It's quick, healthy, and full of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1 green bell pepper (optional)
  • 200g feta cheese
  • A handful of Kalamata olives (or balck olives)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Chop veggies: Cut cucumber, tomatoes, and bell pepper into bite-sized pieces. Slice the red onion thinly.
    2. Combine: Put all chopped veggies in a large bowl.
    3. Add cheese & olives: Cut feta into cubes (or crumble) and add to the bowl. Toss in olives.
    4. Drizzle & season: Pour olive oil over the salad. Sprinkle oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix gently.
    5. Serve: Enjoy fresh!

Tip: For extra flavor, add a splash of lemon juice.
